Closing a referral in SharePoint

To close a case, the liaison lead first needs to follow up with the local organisation, to check that:

  • support the broker agreed was delivered
  • the organisation is satisfied with the outcome of their request for support

The system will prompt the liaison lead to do this once a month until the case is closed. If the organisation is satisfied with the support they received or other outcome of their request, the liaison lead can update the request status to ‘Complete’, which closes the case.

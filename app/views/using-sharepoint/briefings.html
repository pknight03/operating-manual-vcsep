Briefings before your shift

Before each shift you and other operators will have a briefing with your supervisor.

Briefings include:

  1. Daily updates about the support line
  2. Checks operators are ready to answer calls
  3. Opprtunity for operators to ask question before the shift starts

Briefing times and video links

Briefings take place 15 minutes before your shift.

  1. 09:45 - AM shift briefing
  2. 13:45 - PM shift briefing

A Zoom video call link to join briefings will be emailed to you along with other shift details.

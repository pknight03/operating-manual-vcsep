Email templates

Use these templates to make sure you collect and pass on all the information needed to take forward requests for support.

For liaison leads

  • Reply following a review of requests
  • Sharing local brokers contact details with information managers
  • A request cannot be brokered locally
  • Contacting a local organisation to request more information

For information managers

  • Acknowledgement of request for support from local organisations
  • Acknowledgement of request for support from liaison leads
  • Requests for liaison leads to review
  • Request for more information from a local organisation (via liaison lead)
  • Information Governance training for brokers
  • Acknowledgment that support has been found for a local organisation

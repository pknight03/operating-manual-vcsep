Requests for liaison leads to review

Send this email to a liaison lead to share support requests from local organisations ready for their review. We recommend you set up and save this example response as a draft email, and copy it from there each time you need to acknowledge a new request.

Checklist

1. Copy and paste example text below into the email.

2. Replace any text in square brackets with real information.

3. Copy and paste the request reports into the email.

4. Add the Duty Managers contact list.



Dear Liaison Lead

Re: Requests ready for local review

Please review the following requests for support made by local organisations in your local area.

If the request cannot be met locally, notify me by email immediately so that it can be escalated.

If you believe it the request can be answered locally, notify me within the next 24 hours that you are exploring local options.

If the request becomes urgent, alert the Duty Manager for your Regional Tactical Cell.

Many thanks,

Information Manager

'Request support as a voluntary or community organisation' service

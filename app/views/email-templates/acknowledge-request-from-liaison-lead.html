Acknowledging support requests

This is what a liaison lead or local organisation receives within 24 hours when they have submitted a request for support.

Steps

1. Add "Dear [name of organisation]" to top of email.

2. Add "Date received [dd/mm/yy]". This is the date the request arrived in the system.

2. Copy and paste example text below into the email.

3. Copy and paste report output into the email.



Re: Confirmation of your request from the Request support for a voluntary or community organisation service

Thank you for submitting a request through the VCS EP Request support as a voluntary or community organisation service. Your request will now be reviewed. Please allow 10 working days for actions to be assigned to this request.

Below are the details of the request.

Many thanks,

Request support as a voluntary or community organisation service

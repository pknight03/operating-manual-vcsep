Sharing local brokers contact details with information managers

This template is to be used by liaison leads to notifty an information manager a local broker has been found who requires IG training. The broker must also be cc.ed into this email.



Dear Information Manager,

Re: A local broker has been found

I am writing to update you on the request made by [name of organisation] on [full date].

I have now found a local broker who will be taking forward the request for support.

  • Broker contact details
  • Name:
  • Address:
  • Email:
  • Mobile phone number: (for text message reminders)

  • How they can help
  • [Name of broker] told us that they would be able to support by [please enter brief statement of how broker has said they can support with the request]

  • For how long
  • From:
  • To:

  • Your actions
  • Please:
  • 1. Add the broker to our contact list
  • 2. Send them the Information Governance training link

  • They will then be able to access the personally identifiable information they need and make contact with the local organisation to broker support for them.

  • Many thanks,

  • Liaison Lead

Useful contacts for liaison leads

As a liaison lead for the 'Request support as a local organisation' service you should have received a list of all the contact information you need by email after you completed your information governance training.

Your list will include contact details for:

  • liaison leads
  • information managers
  • tactical cell duty managers
  • crisis response

If you've completed the training and have not received the contact list, or if you have issues with how the service is working or with any processes related to the service, contact:

British Red Cross VCSEPservice@RedCross.org.uk

For issues with your role as a liaison lead, contact NAVCA.

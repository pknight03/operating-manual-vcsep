Local infrastructure bodies

Local infrastructure bodies are the voice of the local voluntary and community sector, providing their members with a range of services and development support.

They work with local groups to provide access to information on, for example, training, funding and legal advice.

They also work with new groups to address unmet needs as well as ensuring that existing groups are equipped to shape their services to the changing needs of the community.

Response times

Liaison Leads will contact the organisation requesting support within 24 hours of having received a request for help through the ‘Get help as a local organisation’ service online form.

This does not mean that arrangements for support are made within 24 hours. It is a ‘first contact’ timeframe for Liaison Leads to speak with or respond by email to local organisations: to acknowledge their request for help and ask for further details.

If the request is urgent, each of 5 regional tactical cells has a duty manager available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

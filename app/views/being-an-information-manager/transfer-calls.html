Transfer calls

Some calls you might need to transfer to someone else in your team or another organisation.

Transfer calls to a different service

  1. Press the number ‘1’ on your phone
  2. You will hear: ‘Please enter the target number’.
  3. Enter the phone number for the service you need to transfer the call to

Transfer calls to another BRC operator

  1. Press the number ‘1’ on your phone
  2. You will hear: ‘Please enter the target number’.
  3. Enter the 4 digit agent code for the operator you need to transfer the call to

Set up your phone ready to answer calls

Check what you need to do to set-up your phone ready to answer calls

  1. Step 1 Tell us your phone number

    You can only answer support line calls if you've told us your phone number.

    We ask for your phone number when you sign up as a support line volunteer.

    You only need to tell us your number once.

  2. Step 2 Turn off your voicemail

    Turn off your voice mail, if you're using a mobile phone to answer support line calls.

    When you can't answer calls and your voice mail is still on, people calling the support line may hear the message and get confused.

    1. How to turn off voicemail

  3. Step 3 Hide your phone number

    You will only be recieving support line calls. The number people will see when calling the support line is this number, not yours.

    If you want to hide your phone number, you can:

    1. How to hide your phone number

  4. Step 4 Call the setup phone number

    With the phone you've given us the number for, call this free number:

    0800 4589 480

    We suggest saving this number on your phone.

  5. Step 5 Enter your agent code

    A recorded message will ask you to enter your 'agent code'.

    We will text your 'agent code' before your shift.

    Your agent code is four digits long followed by star. For example, ‘8521*’.

  6. Step 6 Enter the agent pin

    Next you will be asked to enter 'agent pin':

    1234*

    This number is always the same.

  7. Step 7 Update your status to available

    Next you'll be asked to change your status, which means being available or not to answer calls.

    Change your status to available by:

    1. 1. Press 1 on your phone
    2. 2. You'll hear 'Your current status is logged out. To log in press 1'
    3. 3. Press 1 again to login, which changes your status to available
    4. 4. You'll then hear ‘Your status has been updated.’
    5. 5. Finish by pressing 2

  8. Step 8 You are ready to answer calls

    Now you have entered the right information in the right order, you are ready to take calls.

    1. How to answer calls with your phone
    2. Transfer calls
    3. Stop support line calls to your phone

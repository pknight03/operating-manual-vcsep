Triaging as a liaison lead

There are 2 options for new requests for support.

1. Assessing for local support.

Liaison Lead identifies that support needs can be met locally.

2. Escalating request to the Regional Tactical Cell.

This happens when a liaison lead either:

  • has tried to get a request met locally but not been able to
  • knows straight away that a request cannot be met locally
  • Triaging as a liaison lead
  • Brokering local support
  • Escalating a request
  • Making a request on behalf of an organisation

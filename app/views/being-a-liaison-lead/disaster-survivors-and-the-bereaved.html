What personal information to collect

The National Emergencies Support Line only collects the minimum information needed to give people support.

Personal information collected:

  • Name of person calling
  • Number of adults and children affected
  • Whether people are vulnerable, for example pregnant or impaired mobility
  • What support people immediately need. For example temporary accommodation or replacement medicine
  • Home address postcode to refer to local services
  • Location that has been affected
  • Location people are when they call
  • Location people are staying that night
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Contact preference. For example text message or phone call
  • Can we leave a message to follow-up
  • Consent to collect and share information

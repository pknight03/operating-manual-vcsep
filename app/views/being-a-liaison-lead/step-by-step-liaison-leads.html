Step-by-step for liaison leads

This guidance is for when a liaison lead makes a request for support on behalf of a local organisation.

Step 1. Complete information governance training

To get started on SharePoint, complete the information governance training provided by the British Red Cross on the International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies (IFRC) e-learning platform.

Access information governance training instructions

Step 2. Log into SharePoint

When you have completed the training, you will be sent log in details for SharePoint by the VCS EP 'Request support as a voluntary or community organisation' service team.

Step 3. Review new requests from liaison leads

Check there is enough information to move the request forward. If there is not enough information, use the email template to request it from the liaison lead.

Step 4. Escalate the request

Escalate new requests from liaison leads to the Regional Tactical Cell, as the liaison leads as will know the needs cannot be met locally.

Step 5. Produce report of all requests

Your report should only include:

  • location of request area – the Local Resilience Forum area
  • name of organisation
  • details of type and frequency of support

By only including the above, the report will not include any personally identifiable information.

The day before your Regional Tactical Cell meeting, make sure you get your report in by 3pm, otherwise it will be delayed until the next week’s meeting.

Step 6. Email report to Regional Tactical Cell

Share the report ahead of the meeting via email to the Regional Tactical Cell lead.

Step 7. Attend Regional Tactical Cell meeting

Regional Tactical Cell Lead presents the report as part of the meeting.

During the meeting, brokers will consider whether they can respond to a request, based on whether they:

  • already provide this service
  • already work in the target areas
  • have sufficient capacity in the target areas
  • have expertise with this target audience
  • can commit to the timescale of the request

If a broker needs more information follow steps 8, 9 and 10.

Step 9. Email liaison lead to get more information

After the meeting the information manager emails the liaison lead to ask them to approach the local organisation to get more information on the request (include questions asked by the broker in the email).

Practical advice you could give callers

This list of advice is not meant to be exhaustive and they may not always be appropriate. They are just examples of advice that may be helpful.

Practical advice includes:

  1. Set up regular calls with family or friends
  2. Create a written list of your Next of Kin / close family or friends to be contacted in an emergency. Include their names, phone numbers and addresses
  3. Keep your emergency contact list in your wallet
  4. Make sure your emergency contacts know your current phone number and home address

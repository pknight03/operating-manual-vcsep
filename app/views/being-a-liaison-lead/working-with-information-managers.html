Working with information managers

List of Information Managers and areas they cover.

Search by Local Resilience Forum area to find the Information Manager for your area.>

As a Liaison Lead you are supported by an Information Manager who covers different areas. Read more about the Information Manager role.

Main points of communication for you with the information manager are when:

  • They ask you to help them to get more information on a request from an organisation.
  • You ask them to contact brokers with Information Governance training, so that a trained broker can support a locally identified broker by accessing personally identifiable information related to a specific request for help being met locally.

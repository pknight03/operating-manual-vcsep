Working with information managers

As a liaison lead you are supported by an information manager.

Your main points of communication with the information manager:

  • They ask you to help them to get more information on a request from an organisation.
  • You ask them to contact brokers with information governance training to support a locally identified broker, by accessing personally identifiable information related to a specific request for help that is being met locally.

Who the service is for

This service is for grassroots, voluntary and community groups, and other local organisations in England.

Many groups in the UK are already providing outstanding support to their communities, especially during COVID-19. When local organisations need extra support they can also approach their local voluntary service councils, local authorities or local resilience forums.

When to use the service

However, we know that there are times when there is no local capacity to provide the support required. When that happens, local organisations can make a request for support from the new ‘Get support as a local organisation’ service.

Requests are reviewed by local liaison leads, who find help locally or escalate to a regional panel of voluntary and community groups.

'Get support as a local organisation' service

This service is for voluntary, community and other local organisations or groups to make a request for support, after exploring all local options and not finding the required support.

Organisations and groups should first approach their:

  • local council for voluntary service
  • local authority
  • local resilience forum
  • other local infrastructure bodies

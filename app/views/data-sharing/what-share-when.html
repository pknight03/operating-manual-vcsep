What information can you share and when?

To access information that includes personally identifiable information you must complete our Information Governance Training.

Personally identifiable information includes:

  • Names
  • Phone numbers
  • Email addresses

To access information that does not include personally identifiable information, the training does not need to be completed.

Non-personally identifiable information includes:

  • Name of organisation
  • Type of request
  • Scale of request
  • When support is needed
  • What information to collect
  • What not to collect
  • Information Governance Training
  • What information can you share when?

What information can you share and when?

Before anyone can access information that includes personally identifiable information they must complete our Information Governance Training.

Personally identifiable information

Do not share personally identifiable information with anyone who has not completed our training.

Personally identifiable information includes:

  • names
  • phone numbers
  • email addresses

Non-personally identifiable information

To access information that does not include personally identifiable information, our information governance training does not need to be completed. You can share this sort of information with people involved with our service who have not completed the information governance training.

Non-personally identifiable information includes:

  • name of organisation
  • type of request
  • scale of request
  • dates of when support is needed

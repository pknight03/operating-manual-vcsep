What support it offers

The service can help local organisations by:

connecting them with the type of volunteers they need – for example, volunteers who are DBS checked. connecting them to specialist skills or expertise – for example, mental health charities who can provide training in mental health support. signposting them to relevant sources of information - for example, information for available grants for local organisations.

Staying local or escalating

As a liaison lead you have 2 options for progressing new requests for support.

1. Assess and recommend local support.

You identify that support needs can be met locally.

2. Escalate request to the Regional Tactical Cell.

You either:

  • have tried getting a request met locally but not been able to
  • know straight away that a request cannot be met locally

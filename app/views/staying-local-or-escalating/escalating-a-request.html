Escalating a request

Making a request on behalf of a local organisation

Liaison leads can request regional support arrangements for an organisation by completing the ‘Get support as a local organisation’ service online form.

You will need to enter the contact details – name and phone number – of the person at the organisation who will be the main contact.

Escalate after trying to find local support

If as a liaison lead you have tried to broker support locally, and not been able to do so, request an escalation to the regional tactical cell from the information manager, using the escalation email template.
